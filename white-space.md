## ✔️ white-space 
 ---<br/>title: white-space<br/>slug: Web/CSS/white-space<br/>tags:<br/>  - CSS<br/>  - CSS Property<br/>  - CSS Text<br/>  - Reference<br/>  - recipe:css-property<br/>  - white-space<br/>browser-compat: css.properties.white-space<br/>---<br/>{{CSSRef}}<br/><br/>The **`white-space`** CSS property sets how {{Glossary(whitespace, white space)}} inside an element is handled.<br/><br/>{{EmbedInteractiveExample(pages/css/white-space.html)}}<br/><br/>The property specifies two things:<br/><br/>- Whether and how white space is [collapsed](#collapsing_of_white_space).<br/>- Whether lines may wrap at soft-wrap opportunities.<br/><br/>> **Note:** To make words break _within themselves_, use {{CSSxRef(overflow-wrap)}}, {{CSSxRef(word-break)}}, or {{CSSxRef(hyphens)}} instead.<br/><br/>## Syntax<br/><br/>```css<br/>/* Keyword values */<br/>white-space: normal;<br/>white-space: nowrap;<br/>white-space: pre;<br/>white-space: pre-wrap;<br/>white-space: pre-line;<br/>white-space: break-spaces;<br/><br/>/* Global values */<br/>white-space: inherit;<br/>white-space: initial;<br/>white-space: revert;<br/>white-space: revert-layer;<br/>white-space: unset;<br/>```<br/><br/>The `white-space` property is specified as a single keyword chosen from the list of values below.<br/><br/>### Values<br/><br/>- `normal`<br/>  - : Sequences of white space are [collapsed](#collapsing_of_white_space). Newline characters in the source are handled the same as other white space. Lines are broken as necessary to fill line boxes.<br/>- `nowrap`<br/>  - : [Collapses](#collapsing_of_white_space) white space as for `normal`, but suppresses line breaks (text wrapping) within the source.<br/>- `pre`<br/>  - : Sequences of white space are preserved. Lines are only broken at newline characters in the source and at {{HTMLElement(br)}} elements.<br/>- `pre-wrap`<br/>  - : Sequences of white space are preserved. Lines are broken at newline characters, at {{HTMLElement(br)}}, and as necessary to fill line boxes.<br/>- `pre-line`<br/>  - : Sequences of white space are [collapsed](#collapsing_of_white_space). Lines are broken at newline characters, at {{HTMLElement(br)}}, and as necessary to fill line boxes.<br/>- `break-spaces`<br/><br/>  - : The behavior is identical to that of `pre-wrap`, except that:<br/><br/>    - Any sequence of preserved white space always takes up space, including at the end of the line.<br/>    - A line breaking opportunity exists after every preserved white space character, including between white space characters.<br/>    - Such preserved spaces take up space and do not hang, and thus affect the box's intrinsic sizes (min-content size and max-content size).<br/><br/>The following table summarizes the behavior of the various `white-space` values:<br/><br/><table class=standard-table><br/>  <thead><br/>    <tr><br/>      <th></th><br/>      <th>New lines</th><br/>      <th>Spaces and tabs</th><br/>      <th>Text wrapping</th><br/>      <th>End-of-line spaces</th><br/>      <th>End-of-line other space separators</th><br/>    </tr><br/>  </thead><br/>  <tbody><br/>    <tr><br/>      <th><code>normal</code></th><br/>      <td>Collapse</td><br/>      <td>Collapse</td><br/>      <td>Wrap</td><br/>      <td>Remove</td><br/>      <td>Hang</td><br/>    </tr><br/>    <tr><br/>      <th><code>nowrap</code></th><br/>      <td>Collapse</td><br/>      <td>Collapse</td><br/>      <td>No wrap</td><br/>      <td>Remove</td><br/>      <td>Hang</td><br/>    </tr><br/>    <tr><br/>      <th><code>pre</code></th><br/>      <td>Preserve</td><br/>      <td>Preserve</td><br/>      <td>No wrap</td><br/>      <td>Preserve</td><br/>      <td>No wrap</td><br/>    </tr><br/>    <tr><br/>      <th><code>pre-wrap</code></th><br/>      <td>Preserve</td><br/>      <td>Preserve</td><br/>      <td>Wrap</td><br/>      <td>Hang</td><br/>      <td>Hang</td><br/>    </tr><br/>    <tr><br/>      <th><code>pre-line</code></th><br/>      <td>Preserve</td><br/>      <td>Collapse</td><br/>      <td>Wrap</td><br/>      <td>Remove</td><br/>      <td>Hang</td><br/>    </tr><br/>    <tr><br/>      <th><code>break-spaces</code></th><br/>      <td>Preserve</td><br/>      <td>Preserve</td><br/>      <td>Wrap</td><br/>      <td>Wrap</td><br/>      <td>Wrap</td><br/>    </tr><br/>  </tbody><br/></table><br/><br/>> **Note:** There is a distinction made between **spaces** and **other space separators**. These are defined as follows:<br/>><br/>> - spaces<br/>>   - : Spaces (U+0020), tabs (U+0009), and segment breaks (such as newlines).<br/>> - other space separators<br/>>   - : All other space separators defined in Unicode, other than those already defined as spaces.<br/>><br/>> Where white space is said to _hang_, this can affect the size of the box when measured for intrinsic sizing.<br/><br/>## Collapsing of white space<br/><br/>The CSS Text specification contains a [Collapsing and Transformation](https://drafts.csswg.org/css-text/#white-space-phase-1) section that precisely defines what white space is collapsed means, including an example with an illustration. Usually, it means reducing sequences of multiple white-space characters down to a single space character — though in some cases it means reducing them to no character (the empty string).<br/><br/>##
